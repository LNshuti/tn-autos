## Application Overview

Welcome to the documentation for the **Tennessee Auto Repair Businesses Dashboard**. This dashboard provides visualizations and insights into population data, geographical regions, and the locations of various auto repair businesses across the state of Tennessee.

The application includes:

1. **Interactive maps** of Tennessee displaying different geographical layers (Counties, Zip Codes, HSAs, and HRRs).
2. **Population statistics** for the top counties in Tennessee based on the 2010 and 2020 census.
3. **Business locations** mapped by type, such as AutoZone, Napa Auto Parts, O'Reilly Auto Parts, etc.
